Top 5 Strains for Sleep

Get the rest you deserve with these heavy hitters.

Insomnia is one of the most common reasons people turn to cannabis. But not all strains are created equal when it comes to catching Zs. Here are the top 5 Indica strains available for delivery in the Antelope Valley that are perfect for nighttime use.

1. Granddaddy Purple (GDP)

This classic indica is famous for its berry aroma and powerful relaxation effects. It's a staple for sleep.

2. Northern Lights

Specific for its ability to melt away stress and ease the body into a deep state of relaxation.

3. Bubba Kush

Heavy tranquilizing effects make this a go-to for those who need serious help falling asleep.

4. OG Kush

While a hybrid, many OG phenotypes lean heavy into the body, providing a sedative effect perfect for the end of the day.

5. Wedding Cake

Rich and tangy, this strain provides a relaxing euphoria that often leads to a great night's sleep.
